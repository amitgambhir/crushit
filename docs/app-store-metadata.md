## Description (4000 chars max)

CrushIt turns everyday tasks into a rewarding game for the whole family. Parents assign tasks, kids complete them to earn Crush Points, and points unlock real-world rewards you choose.

HOW IT WORKS

Parents create a family, add kids, and set up tasks with point values. Kids see their tasks, mark them done, and submit for approval. Parents review and approve — and the kid's screen instantly erupts with confetti and a points burst. No refreshing, no waiting. It just works.

FOR PARENTS
- Create and assign tasks with custom point values
- Approve or reject completed tasks with one tap
- Set up real rewards your kids care about (screen time, treats, outings)
- Track weekly completion rates and points awarded per child
- See pending approvals and recent family activity at a glance
- Lock parent sections behind a PIN for shared devices

FOR KIDS
- See today's tasks in a clean, fun dashboard
- Earn Crush Points for every approved task
- Level up from Rookie Crusher to Full Legend across 20+ levels
- Build daily streaks and unlock surprise milestone rewards
- Browse the reward store and redeem points for real rewards
- Collect achievement badges and track progress in the trophy room

BUILT FOR FAMILIES
- Each family's data is completely isolated — no one else can see it
- Kids log in with a family name, username, and PIN — no email needed
- Parents control everything: tasks, rewards, approvals, and kid accounts
- Works on iPhone with a dark-themed, kid-friendly interface

GAMIFICATION THAT MOTIVATES
Kids don't just check off tasks — they level up, build streaks, unlock badges, and earn surprise rewards at streak milestones. The instant celebration when a parent approves a task creates a feedback loop that keeps kids engaged day after day.

PRIVACY FIRST
- No ads, ever
- No tracking or profiling
- COPPA and GDPR compliant
- Delete all family data anytime
- Full data export available

CrushIt is the app that finally makes "did you do your chores?" a question of the past.
